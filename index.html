Profile & Bio

I'm Ashnat Time (Aysha), a compassionate Community Health Extension Worker (RCHP) focused on preventive care, maternal & child health, and community health education. I believe that healthy communities begin with informed households and culturally respectful care.

Currently studying at Alma'arif College Of Nursing Midwifery and Health Sciences, I collaborate with multidisciplinary teams to deliver person‑centred services across primary healthcare settings.

Career Goals

Advance as a frontline public health practitioner with specialization in Reproductive, Maternal, Newborn, and Child Health. In the near term, I aim to lead community outreach programs that improve immunization uptake and ANC coverage in underserved areas.
